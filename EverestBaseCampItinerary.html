Everest Base Camp Trek

About The Place

The Everest Base Camp (EBC) trek is a spectacular high altitude trek in the mountains of Nepal. Prepare for jaw-dropping scenery and a unique cultural experience exploring the challenging trekking routes around the highest mountain in the world.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 - Kathmandu to Lukla(2860m,flight) - Phakding(2835m)

    4/5hr Guest House
    • Departure from Tribhuvan International Airport
    • Arrive at Lukla Airport (2860m)
    • Trek to Phakding (2,610m)
    • Transfer to hotel and dinner at hotel
    • Overnight stay at hotel

  • Day 2 - Phakding(2835m) - Namche Bazaar(3440m)

    3-4 hours Guest House
    • Early morning trek to Namche Bazzar(3,445m)
    • After reaching Namche Bazzar transfer to hotel
    • Sightseeing around Golden Mountains during sunset
    • Dinner at hotel
    • Overnight stay at hotel

  • Day 3: Namche Bazaar(3440m) - Tengboche(3930m)

    2-3 hours Guest House
    • Early morning trek begins to Tengboche(3860m) through narrow trails
    • Lunch en-route
    • Enjoy the outstanding view of mountains during trek
    • Tengboche Monastery sightseeing
    • Dinner & overnight at hotel

  • Day 4: Tengboche(3930) - Dingboche(4410)

    2-3 hours Guest House
    • Trek begins to Dingboche(4410m)
    • Trek through rhododendron forest and gradual steep climbs
    • Enjoy the magical view of different mountains during the trek
    • Lunch en-route
    • Sightseeing around Dingboche valley
    • Dinner & overnight stay at hotel

  • Day 5: Dingboche(4410m) - Lobuche(4940m)

    2-3 hours Guest House
    • Early morning trek to Lobuche (4940m)
    • Upwards climbs and walk into grassland area
    • Lunch en-route
    • Visit to khagan chorten and see the different colorful prayer flag
    • Dinner & overnight stay at hotel

  • Day 6: Lobuche(4940m) - Gorakshep(5164m) - EBC(5364m) - Gorakshep

    2-3 hours Guest House
    • Breakfast in hotel
    • Trek to Gorakshep (5164m) and Everest Base Camp(5364m)
    • Trail will be smoothly up and down
    • Lunch en-route
    • Sightseeing of ghumbu Glacier and mountains
    • Dinner & overnight stay at hotel

  • Day 7: Gorakshep(5164m) - Kalapathar(5643m) - Lobuche(4930m) - Pheriche(4371m)

    2-3 hours Guest House
    • Breakfast in hotel
    • Trek to Kala Pathar(5643m)
    • Lunch en-route
    • see the stunning view of Mt Everest closely
    • Trek back to Gorakshep and descend to Pheriche(4371m)
    • Evening sightseeing around Pheriche
    • Dinner & overnight stay at Pheriche

  • Day 8: Pheriche(4371m) - Namche Bazaar(3440m)

    2-3 hours Guest House
    • Early morning trek back to namche bazzar
    • Youâ€™ll get a perfect scenario of breathtaking mountains vistas
    • Sightseeing around Namche
    • Dinner & overnight stay at Namche Bazzar

  • Day 9: Namche Bazaar(3440m) - Lukla(2860m)

    2-3 hours Guest House
    • After breakfast back down to the small airstrip at Lukla
    • Enjoy the amazing view of Lukla
    • Dinner & overnight stay at Lukla

  • Day 10: Lukla(2860m) - Kathmandu(1400m)

    2-3 hours Guest House
    • Early morning breakfast at hotel
    • Morning sightseeing around Lukla
    • Departure from Lukla airport
    • Arrive to Kathmandu airport
